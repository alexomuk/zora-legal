5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies — you can only access your own private data
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • We do not store payment card details — all payments are handled by Stripe or Google Play

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile, check-ins, habit logs, posts, and comments are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymised aggregated statistics may be retained for analytical purposes
  • We may retain certain data for longer if required by law (e.g. financial records for up to 7 years)

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at alexomuk@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app:

  1. Go to Profile → Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing alexomuk@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including check-ins, posts, and profile information, will be permanently removed.

9. Push Notifications

We use push notifications to send you reminders and community activity updates. You can control push notifications:

  • In the app: Profile → Settings → Push Notifications
  • In your device settings: disable notifications for the Zora app at any time

If you disable notifications, your push token remains stored but will not be used to send notifications until you re-enable them.

10. Children's Policy

Zora is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at alexomuk@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The Zora mobile app does not use cookies. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites. We may collect anonymised usage analytics to help us understand how the app is used and to improve it. This data cannot be used to identify you individually.

12. International Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (including Supabase and Anthropic) may process data outside the UK or European Economic Area. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UK GDPR, such as standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions.

13. Changes to This Policy

When we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
