Delete Your Zora Account

You have the right to delete your Zora account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted and how to request deletion.

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and all data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile and personal information (name, username, email)
  • All your check-ins and streak data
  • Your posts, comments and reactions
  • Your challenge progress and badges
  • Your habit tracking logs
  • Your saved preferences and notification settings
  • All other data associated with your account

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Zora app on your device
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap Settings ⚙️
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete My Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be signed out.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email us to delete your account

Send to alexomuk@gmail.com with subject: Delete My Account
Please include your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data retention

Upon deletion, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Aggregated, anonymised statistics (with no personally identifying information) may be retained for analytics purposes.
